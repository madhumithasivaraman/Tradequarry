Commodities trading

Commodities trading entails trading raw materials like gold, oil, or agricultural products, with profits influenced by market demand, geopolitical events, and economic factors. Forex trading

Forex trading entails speculating on currency pair price movements in the global foreign exchange market, offering opportunities for profit based on fluctuations driven by various economic and geopolitical factors. It operates 24/5, providing high liquidity and potential for gains or losses.
