Trading Charts
Trading charts serve as visual representations of price movements in financial markets, aiding traders in analyzing trends and making informed decisions based on historical data.

Mountain chart

Mountain charts are visually depict price trends resembling a mountain shape and aiding traders in strategic decision-making.
